Birdhouses
Facts
Some of the key elements to getting a bird to move into your birdhouse are patience, location and height.
Location
You will want to use a safe shaded location preferably facing the east. Although this is not a proven fact it is pretty common that a bird will take to a birdhouse which recieves natural sunlight in the morning.
Height
You will want to keep the bird house atleast 5 feet off of the ground. The height is not a problem as long as you stick to 5-12 feet off of the ground. Dont be afraid to move our birdhouse up or down to atract a nice bird.
